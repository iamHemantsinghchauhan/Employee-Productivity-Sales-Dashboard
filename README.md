# Employee Productivity & Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee productivity, attrition patterns, workforce demographics, and performance metrics.

The objective of this project is to transform raw HR data into actionable insights that help organizations understand employee behavior, identify attrition drivers, and support data-driven HR decision making.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition across different workforce factors
- Identify the impact of overtime and business travel on employee turnover
- Understand workforce distribution across age groups and demographics
- Evaluate employee performance and satisfaction trends
- Provide insights to support HR strategy and workforce planning

## 📈 Key Insights
- The overall employee attrition rate is **16.12%** out of a total workforce of **1000 employees**.
- Employees working **overtime show significantly higher attrition** compared to those who do not work overtime.
- **Frequent business travelers experience higher turnover**, suggesting work-life balance challenges.
- The **26–35 age group shows the highest attrition**, indicating higher mobility among early-career professionals.
- The organization has a **young workforce**, with most employees between **18–35 years**.
- **Average salary is 112.96K** with an **average employee age of 28.99 years**, reflecting a relatively young and competitive workforce.
- Employee satisfaction metrics such as job satisfaction, environment satisfaction, and work-life balance remain **relatively stable across years**.
